Role Name: Market Intelligence Agent

Brand Title : Market Rover - Crew Intelligence Agent 

Mission Statement: Provide a Weekly summary of the three most important news stories affecting the user’s stock portfolio and highlight potential risks.

The Brain

Strategy: ReAct (Reasoning + Acting).

Logic: 1. Retrieve user’s stock list from the database/excel/csv. 2. Search for news in the last one week for each stock. 3. Reason whether the news is "Negative," "Positive," or "Neutral." 4. Sentiment: Analyze if global market cues (Nifty/sector the specific stock belongs to) are positive/negative. 5. Generate a summary.

The Hands

yfinance (Python): For free daily data (e.g., RELIANCE.NS for NSE, RELIANCE.BO for BSE).

Kite Connect API (Zerodha): The gold standard for retail trade data and execution in India. (Alternates: SmartAPI by Angel One or Upstox API).

NewsAPI (India Filter): Filtered for "Business" category in India.

Python Newspaper3k: A library to "scrape" and summarize articles from Moneycontrol or Financial Express if they don't have an open API.

Safeguards

Max Iterations: 15

Human Gate: None (Passive briefing), but provide a "Flag for Review" button if the sentiment analysis is uncertain.

Data Guard: Cross-verify yfinance data with NSEPython library to ensure accuracy.
